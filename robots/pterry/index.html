Robots - Pterry

Pterry was the robot that our team, 6422 the Gearheadz, built for the 2019 FIRST Robotics Competition. This year I was the lead mechanic.

The goal of the game was to pick up the orange kickballs and acrylic disks, and place them on/in the ports of the rocket.

The video to the left shows Pterry reaching up to place a kickball in the top goal. I built most of the dual stage elevator that allows it to get up so high.


To the far right is Pterry (Red bumpers, 6422) placing an acrylic hatch panel on the middle stage of the rocket.

Immediately to the right is a video of the intake/box, which got lifted by the elevator.
