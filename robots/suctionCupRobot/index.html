Suction Cup Robot

The suction cup robot was a quick project meant as a proof of concept. Originally I wanted to make a bot that could climb a window, but eventually aimed to get it to walk across a surface.

It has a servo-controlled suction cup on each foot, that could then swing around. The idea was to have it suction to the ground with one leg, then swing and reach forward, then release and grab with the other suction cup, allowing it to walk across a smooth surface.

It was made with 3D printed parts, SG90 servos, and two syringes.

Suction Cup Demonstration

The robot is controlled by an Arduino uno.
