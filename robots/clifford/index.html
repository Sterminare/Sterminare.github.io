Clifford the Ro-bot Dog

Clifford is my second revision of my ongoing robot dog project. It is a large improvement over Goddard (version 1) in many ways. Principally, the dog has been greatly miniaturized to reduce weight and the force needed by the servos. Additionally, all of the servos have been placed on the chassis of the dog, so the arms have been further lightened. To accomplish this, it uses a linkage inspired by 'Kangal' by Baris Alp.

Clifford is documented further and all files are available at its github repository.
