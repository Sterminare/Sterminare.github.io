Robots - Carry

Carry was the robot that our team, 6422 the Gearheadz, built for the 2020 FIRST Robotics Competition. This year I was the team Captain, and would have been the driver. Unfortunately the competition was cancelled just weeks before we got to compete.

The goal of the game was to pick up the yellow power cells, and shoot them into receptacles, then turn a color wheel, then hang from the balance beam in the center.

The video to the left shows our mechanism for climbing. The robot needed to reach up that high to put the hook onto the balance beam, then the hook (affectionately called the 'BIG BOY') would retract its cable and winch the robot into the air (Which is shown in the video below).


To the right is the intake, which deployed with pneumatic cylinders and rotated to suck in power cells. The purple and white wheels were 3D printed by me, and drag the ball sideways toward the center.
